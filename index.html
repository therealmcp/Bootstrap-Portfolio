About Colin

Colin was born and raised in the Great Pacific Northwest before moving south where the desert sun forged him like steel into the man before you. He is a passionate individual and meets challenges with the ferocity of Bruce Banner seeing a loved one in danger.

After managing a climbing gym I worked as a personal assistant for Hollywood executives until I had saved enough money to open a small business of my own. A passion project with a friend, Car Friend is a small auto shop that puts honesty and the community ahead of making a quick buck. In 2017 I moved back to the beautiful Pacific Northwest to start another business with my brother-in-law which lead to finding my passion at the keyboard.

In August of 2018 I finally found myself in a program that kept me motivated to consistently hone my skills with various programming languages.
